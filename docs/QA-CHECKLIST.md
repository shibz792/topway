# QA Checklist

- Production build and TypeScript pass.
- All routes, navigation, phone, email and WhatsApp links work.
- Employer, candidate and contact forms validate and submit.
- Mobile menu traps focus, closes with Escape and locks body scroll.
- No overflow at required widths; 44px touch targets.
- Keyboard focus is visible; headings and landmarks are semantic.
- Licence image opens and remains readable.
- Reduced motion disables non-essential movement.
- No unsupported market, vacancy, guarantee or licence claims.
- Metadata, sitemap, robots and structured data remain valid.
